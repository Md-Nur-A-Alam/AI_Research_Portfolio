About Me

I am a final-year Computer Science and Engineering student at Bangladesh Army University of Science and Technology (BAUST) with a CGPA of 3.95. My passion lies in advancing Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing, with a focus on creating meaningful technology for Bengali speakers. I have led tech communities, excelled in competitive programming, and contributed to research and teaching.
